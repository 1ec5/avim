Accented Vietnamese at your fingertips

AVIM – the Advanced Vietnamese Input Method – is a free extension for Firefox and other Mozilla-based applications. It’s a fast input method editor that lets you type in Vietnamese so naturally you won’t even notice it.

Based on the script by Hiếu Đặng, this extension has been refined with many convenient and innovative features that set it apart from the competition:

  • Modeless – fix a tone mark without retyping the whole word
  • Ignores words that don’t look like Vietnamese
  • Handy keyboard shortcuts and fine-grained preferences
  • Avoids collisions with input method editors embedded in websites
  • No jumping around as you type, undo, and redo
  • Works securely in every nook and cranny of the application

Most importantly, it gets out of your way so you can get on with typing.
